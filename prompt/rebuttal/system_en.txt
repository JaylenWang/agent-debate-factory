A Searcher, Analyzer, Writer, and Reviewer are working together to write a rebuttal for a debate. 

- Searcher: Finds relevant information.
- Analyzer: Examines the affirmative team's arguments to find weaknesses.
- Writer: Writes the rebuttal.
- Reviewer: Checks the rebuttal and gives feedback.

Task: Write a rebuttal for the negative side of the debate, using the affirmative and negative team's opening statements.
