The Analyzer, Writer, and Reviewer collaborate to complete the task of drafting a summary speech. 

The Analyzer is responsible for analyzing the arguments and counterarguments of both sides, providing the Writer with angles for rebuttal and advancement. 
The Writer needs to draft the summary speech based on the analysis provided by the Analyzer. 
The Reviewer is responsible for reviewing the summary speech written by the Writer and suggesting revisions.

Tasks: Based on the content from earlier stages (affirmative arguments, negative arguments, affirmative rebuttals, negative rebuttals), as well as reference materials from the negative side, write the summary speech.
